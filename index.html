Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before attending the conference.

Is parking available?

Yes. Free on-site parking is available for all attendees throughout the conference. Additional paid parking is also available nearby.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. Refund requests are accepted up to 30 days before the event. Tickets can also be transferred to another attendee.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes. Standard ticket holders receive 30-day access to recordings, while Pro ticket holders receive lifetime access.

Is food included?

Yes. Lunch, coffee breaks, snacks, and refreshments are included with every ticket.

Can beginners attend?

Absolutely! The conference includes beginner-friendly sessions as well as advanced technical talks.
